Meet The Founders

Komal Mann

Founder and President

Komal is final-year Computer Science Engineering Student who loves exploring different technologies. She is hugely passionate about demystifying "Computer Science" to people with little or no exposure to the powerful world of TECH. With a knack for Public Speaking, she organises & hosts student-oriented global events to engage with Industry experts, and connect them to curious learners around the world.

Rishabh Raj

Lead Advisor

Rishabh raj, currently in final year, filed 28+ patents, chairperson @IEEE Chandigarh University student branch, Lead @ Events CSE, President @ innovator's hub, Campus Representative @ Meta developer's Chandigarh, delivered 150+ sessions across india, Lead advisor @ z Students powered by IBM Z and CU.

We feel glad to share that our CSE Student, Komal Mann has been selected as the #1 IBM Z Ambassador Of The Year 2021.

Ms Mann's has been persistent with outstanding efforts at the IBM Z Ambassador Program and also represented the entire APAC region as a CU Student at IBM Z’s annual tech conference — IBM Z Day 2021. Also, with the constant support and guidance from the CU Faculty, Ms Mann also established zStudents-CU, the world’s first-ever student community powered by IBM Z, with the motive to accelerate the development of technical & soft skills and more importantly, to make learning opportunities more accessible to students. Her efforts also earned her recognition as 'IBM Z Rising Star' by TechChannel' by TechChannel.

We congratulate Ms Komal Mann on her achievement.

About Us

World's First Ever Community Powered By IBM Z

With a mission to accelerate the development of both technical & interpersonal skills in students, zStudents-CU is entirely with students, for students, and by students! The community focuses on enabling students to better discover what the future of work needs by connecting them to Industry Experts through virtually hosted sessions. With support from IBM, the zStudents-CU Team strives to bring valuable content to life, and make it accessible to proactive learners around the world via IBM Z Global Student Hub.
